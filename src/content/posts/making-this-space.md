This site starts as a simple place to write things down before they get scattered across chats, notes, bookmarks, and half-finished drafts.

I expect the topics to move between software, systems, cooking, travel, and everyday problem solving. The common thread is usefulness: what did I learn, what changed my mind, what would I want to remember the next time I face the same problem?

The design will keep evolving, but the purpose is already clear. This is a personal knowledge base in public.
