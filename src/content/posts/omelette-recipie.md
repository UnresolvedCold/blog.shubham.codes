2 eggs + 10 ml Milk + Black pepper + Salt 

![Egg mixture](/images/uploads/20260719_221901.jpg "small center")

Mix them well then heat the pan for 2 mins

![Heating the pan](/images/uploads/20260719_222159.jpg "small center")

![Omelette mixture in pan](/images/uploads/20260719_222324.jpg "small center")

Put the mixture in the pan and cove the lid for 2 mins

![Covered pan](/images/uploads/20260719_222412.jpg "small center")

Uncover the lid and cook uncovered till the mixture is cooked enough 

![Cooked omelette](/images/uploads/20260719_223143.jpg "small center")
